Build your Career with ICTAK

ICT Academy of Kerala (ICTAK) is a social enterprise officially launched on the 24th of June, 2014. The organization had a humble beginning providing skill training programs to selected academic institutions. Over the years, ICTAK has grown to a prime service provider of all ICT and innovation-related training and capacity-building programs in the state.ICT Academy of Kerala provides the best quality education to upskill the youths across the state in various sectors.
